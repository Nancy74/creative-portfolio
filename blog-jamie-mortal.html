Creating Jamie Mortal: A Multi-Modal AI Music Experiment

In July 2025, I launched a small creative AI experiment: building a fictional indie artist persona called Jamie Mortal from scratch, using AI tools for music, visuals, voice and video.

Project Overview

The goal wasn’t to go viral or build a fanbase — it was to understand what’s creatively possible with today’s AI tools. I wanted to test how these tools could work together across different media: sound, image, video, and storytelling.

How Jamie Was Made

  • Visuals: I used Midjourney to create original images of Jamie, and Photoshop with Generative Expand to refine them.
  • Design: I created Jamie’s album and single covers in Canva, using background removal, font, graphics and layout tools.
  • Music: I composed and generated songs in Suno — lyrics, vocals, and mixing all done via AI prompts and refinements.
  • Voice: Jamie’s speaking voice was generated in Eleven Labs and used for short video monologues and introductions.
  • Video: I edited and captioned videos for social media using Hedra AI, CapCut for short-form videos and DaVinci Resolve for long-form, with a focus on subtitled content for viewers who watch without sound.

Creative Learning from the Project

This experiment pushed me to explore:

  • Prompt engineering across image, voice, and music tools
  • Multi-step creative workflows using AI and human edits
  • Brand storytelling and persona development through multimedia
Jamie Mortal hasn't gone viral yet! But he represents what’s now possible when creative direction and AI tools combine.
