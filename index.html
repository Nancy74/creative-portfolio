AI Artist Experiment: Jamie Mortal

In July 2025, I created a small but impactful creative experiment — an AI-generated indie-pop persona named Jamie Mortal. I used Midjourney for the visuals, Suno for the music, and Eleven Labs for voiceovers, allowing Jamie to 'speak' directly to audiences on social media.

This project gave me hands-on experience in multi-modal storytelling and helped me refine practical skills in prompt design, audio control and digital identity building.
